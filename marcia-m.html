Project Description:
CodeGotchi is a retro-style Tamagotchi game where you guide a digital developer through the ups and downs of dev life. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it features interactive gameplay, stats management, localStorage, and pixel-style visuals. Can you help your dev level up?!
